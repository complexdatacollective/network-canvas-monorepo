Before you build a protocol or run an interview, it helps to plan how your study
will move through the Network Canvas tools from end to end. Whichever tools you
choose, the shape of a study is the same: **design** a protocol, **collect** data
by running it, and **export** that data for analysis.

## Before you start collecting data

Planning a study is more than choosing a workflow. Two topics are worth reviewing
early, because they shape how you design your protocol and handle participant data:

- **[GDPR Compliance Guide](/en/get-started/planning-a-study/gdpr-compliance)** — how Interviewer and Fresco handle participant data and what your responsibilities are.
- **[IRB and Security Best Practices](/en/get-started/planning-a-study/irb-best-practices)** — how to think about data security and institutional review when working with sensitive data.
