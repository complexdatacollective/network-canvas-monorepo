`FamilyPedigree`: the checklist now nudges you to record a partner's parents
once that partner has had children with ego. A partner who contributes to the
next generation has family history relevant to those children, so their parents
are prompted — optionally, like the grandparents nudge, and only when the
partner is a genetic (biological/donor) co-parent.
